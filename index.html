How to play?

Rules:

The goal of the game is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over 21.
Each player is dealt one card to start the game and each one is given the opportunity to hit or stand.
If a player hits, he will be dealt another card.
If he stands, he will not be dealt another card.
Face cards (Jack, Queen and King) are worth 10, Aces are worth 11, and all other cards are worth their face value.
If a player goes over 21 he busts. he only wins when all other players are busted or he has a hand value closer to 21 than any other player who hasn't busted. In case of a tie between two or more players, it's simply considered a "Draw".

The use of the masculine pronoun in this writing is purely conventional and does not carry any discriminatory connotations.
